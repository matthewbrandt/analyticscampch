About this event

We're back with our large yearly gathering of the analytics community!

The full-day AnalyticsCamp is an event to allow as many people from our community to exchange knowledge and have fun. As usual, there are no set speakers - everyone is welcome to bring a prepared presentation, discussion topic, problem, or decide on the spot to host a session!

This event takes place on Friday September 27th, 2024 at the offices of Ergon Informatik AG in the heart of Zurich, just a short walk from the Zurich Stadelhofen train station.

Tickets

This is a paid event but as usual, we only ask for a small charge in order to cover some of our costs. Tickets will be available soon - sign up to our newsletter to receive a notification when ticketing opens!
