About this event

We haven't had an event for some time due to COVID-19, but now we're back!

As our first event of 2023, we'll be hosting another Mini-AnalyticsCamp in order to continue to allow as many people from our community to attend an AnalyticsCamp of any size.

In contrast to the full-day AnalyticsCamp, this event takes place in the evening on Monday August 28th, 2023 at the EspaceLab - Swiss Post Innovation space directly in the offices of the Swiss Post!

The provisional programme* will be as follows:

  • 16h00 - 16h30: Welcome
  • 16h30 - 17h00: Intro & Session Planning
  • Short Break
  • 17h10 - 17h55: Session Block 1
  • Short Break
  • 18h10 - 19h55: Session Block 2
  • 20h00 - 21h00: Food, drinks & networking
